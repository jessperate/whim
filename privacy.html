Whim · The fine print

Privacy policy

Effective 5 July 2026 · whim-eta.vercel.app

Whim is a pocket concierge for Paris. It works best when it knows your taste, where you're standing, and what the sky is doing — so it handles personal data. Here is all of it, in plain language.

What we collect

  • Your taste file — answers to the taste questions, swipes, saves (hearts), and reviews you write.
  • Your profile — a name, an optional photo, an optional short bio, and a handle if you create an account.
  • Your location — only while the app is open, only if you grant it, and only to measure distances to places and fetch nearby suggestions. It is never stored on our servers.
  • Concierge chats — messages you send the concierge, kept on your device so the conversation has memory.

Where it lives

Without an account: everything stays in your browser's local storage, on your device. Nothing is uploaded.

With an account: your taste file and profile sync to our database (hosted by Supabase) so they survive a lost phone and power the friends features.

Who can see what

  • Strangers: nothing. Database rules block anyone who isn't you from reading your data.
  • Accepted friends — and only accepted friends — can see your photo, name, bio, the places you've liked, your saved lists, and your reviews. Unfriending someone cuts their access immediately.
  • Your handle and display name are findable by exact search, so friends can add you.

Your contacts

The "invite your contacts" feature runs entirely on your phone: the contact picker and the message it composes never touch our servers. We do not upload, read, or store your address book. Ever.

Services we lean on

  • Google Sign-In for accounts (governed by Google's own policy).
  • Google Places for ratings, photos and discovery — coordinate queries pass through our server, without your identity attached.
  • OpenAI powers the concierge — your chat messages and taste context are sent to it to compose replies.
  • Open-Meteo for weather (coordinates only).
  • Vercel hosts the app and keeps standard, short-lived server logs.

What we don't do

  • No ads, no trackers, no analytics pixels.
  • No selling or renting data to anyone, in any form.
  • No reading your data ourselves except to fix a problem you report.

Deleting your data

"Wipe my taste file" in the You tab erases everything on your device. To delete a synced account and every row attached to it, email jessicarosenb@gmail.com and it will be removed within 30 days.

Changes

If this policy changes, the date at the top changes with it. Material changes get an in-app notice, not a silent edit.
